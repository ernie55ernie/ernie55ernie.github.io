Abstract

Continued from the last post Trip to Mount. Fuji - Kameari Park and Ueno Onshi Park, after we left our hotel in Ueno, we headed to the MRT station. It was already 9:42 when we arrived.

Since we planned to hang in Akihabara for a day, we went to Ikkakuya near Akihabara station to eat breakfast.

I ordered a garlic ramen.

We kept finding advertisement like this.

One of my classmate felt like heaven here.

It is the famous R20!

We had our lunch at Gansozushi.

There are lots of comic related product in Akihabara.

We spent a bonch time looking for interesting gacha.

After walking around for whole day, we left Akihabara.

We had our dinner at Hinaiya in Ginza. The Hennessy berry here is good to drink.

The price of food in Ginza is quite high. As a result, we only ate a little and bought instant noodles and beer near our hotel in Ueno instead.
