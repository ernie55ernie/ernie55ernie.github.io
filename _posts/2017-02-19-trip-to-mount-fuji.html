Abstract

Continued from the last post Trip to Mount. Fuji - Akihabara and Ginza, finally it was the last day of our trip in Japan.

Shibuya

We decided to go to Shibuya and take a look at the famous crossing in front of its station.

We had some udon noodle and miso soup for lunch.

The pork rice here is also good.

There are some interesting advertisements on street in Shibuya.

We visited a shop that sells bath bombs, soaps, and fragrances.

The clerk was very friendly.

Tokyo University

We hung around to see the hospital of Tokyo university.

There was an poster propaganda about reporting perverts.

I had okonomiyaki and a beer as the last dinner of this trip.

It was time to say goodbye to Japan!
