Top Headlines (What & Why It Matters)

- US Digital Markets Restructure Act input & Project Crypto harmonization: The SEC‚Äôs Crypto Task Force is taking written input on a proposed ‚ÄúDigital Markets Restructure Act of 2026‚Äù, which would create a uniform federal framework for issuance, trading, custody, and supervision of digital assets. Near term, this increases headline and rulemaking risk but points to clearer medium‚Äëterm US market structure.[^22][^23][^24][^25][^26]
Risk: High
- MiCA moves into operational phase: The Czech National Bank issued the first six authorisations for crypto‚Äëasset service providers under MiCA on 11 Feb 2026, out of 248 applications, confirming that CASP licensing under the EU regime is now live and supervised. This is a concrete step from abstract regulation to actual licensed infrastructure and will gradually reshape which venues can serve EU clients.[^27]
Risk: Medium
- UK crypto regime consultation deadline (today): The UK FCA‚Äôs three consultation papers on the future cryptoasset regime (CP25/40, CP25/41, CP25/42) have a response deadline of 12 Feb 2026, with final rules expected later this year. Proposals cover trading platforms, intermediaries, staking/DeFi, prudential rules, and a crypto market‚Äëabuse regime, setting up a MiCA‚Äëlike but UK‚Äëspecific framework.[^28][^29][^30]
Risk: Medium
- Stablecoin and RWA infrastructure push: Tether Investments has announced strategic investments in Anchorage Digital and interoperability protocol LayerZero Labs (6 and 10 Feb), alongside a new USD‚ÇÆ Directory mapping real‚Äëworld usage and an investment in t‚Äë0, a USD‚ÇÆ‚Äëpowered settlement network for licensed FIs. OKX Ventures, Hamilton Lane, Securitize and STBL jointly announced an RWA‚Äëbacked stablecoin on OKX‚Äôs X Layer, connecting tokenized private credit with programmable settlement. These moves deepen the linkage between stablecoins, tokenized RWAs and regulated infrastructure.[^31][^32][^33][^34]
Risk: Medium
- Aztec Network TGE and multi‚Äëvenue listings (today): Aztec Network‚Äôs token generation event is scheduled for 12 Feb 2026 at 07:00 UTC (15:00 Beijing), with tokens unlocked via a Uniswap ETH/AZTEC pool seeded with roughly 4,234.6 ETH and 273M AZTEC and an initial on‚Äëchain price around 0.00001551 ETH per AZTEC. Regulation / Law

- US SEC & CFTC alignment (Project Crypto): The 29 Jan joint ‚ÄúHarmonization: U.S. Financial Leadership in the Crypto Era‚Äù event, detailed in client alerts on 1‚Äì11 Feb, signals that the SEC and CFTC will jointly advance Project Crypto, with a shared taxonomy distinguishing digital commodities, collectibles, tools and securities, and an explicit pivot toward prospective rulemaking over enforcement‚Äëonly policy. Research / White Papers (last ~14 days)

- Sentiment ‚Äúextremity premium‚Äù: A 31 Jan 2026 academic paper documents that extreme Crypto Fear & Greed index readings (both fear and greed) are associated with significantly higher bid‚Äìask spreads and an ‚Äúextremity premium‚Äù in BTC, ETH and other large‚Äëcap markets, even after controlling for realized volatility. The work suggests that sentiment intensity‚Äîrather than direction alone‚Äîdrives uncertainty‚Äëlinked liquidity withdrawal, reinforcing the caution signaled by today‚Äôs extreme‚Äëfear readings.[^48]
