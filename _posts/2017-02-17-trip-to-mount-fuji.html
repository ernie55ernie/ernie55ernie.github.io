Abstract

Continued from the last post Trip to Mount. Fuji - Asakusa and Tokyo Skytree, we kept wandering in Tokyo.

Kameari Park

First, we came to Kameari Park where there are lots of status of characters in Kochikame.

We took several photos with Reiko Katherine Akimoto and Keiichi Nakagawa.

Accidentally, we ran into our senior in Kameari Park.

Ueno Onshi Park

Next to Ueno station, Ueno park is a large public park in central Toyko. We took several photos with pedestrians.

Kiyomizu Kannon-dō is the oldest temple in Tokyo.

Saigō Takamori Statue

We also saw the statue of Saigō Takamori, one of the most influential samural in all of Japan.

Inside the park, there is an introduction board.

We met several cute students.
