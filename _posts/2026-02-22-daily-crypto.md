- {Medium (IoTeX), Low (systemic)} IoTeX’s cross-chain bridge suffered a private-key exploit on 21 Feb 2026, when an attacker gained control over the TokenSafe and MinterPool contracts between roughly 07:00 and 09:00 UTC (original reports already in UTC) and drained funds. On-chain sleuths and coverage from Coinpedia/MEXC estimate initial losses of about 4.3 million USD in assets including USDC, USDT, IOTX, PAYG, WBTC, and BUSD, plus roughly 4 million USD in minted CIOTX and 4.5 million USD in CCS, pushing gross damage estimates toward 8–9 million USD.[^3][^5][^14]
- The attacker swapped the bulk of the haul into ETH and bridged a portion (~45 ETH) to BTC via THORChain, complicating recovery, while IoTeX co‑founder Raullen Chai stated that exchanges were coordinating to freeze addresses and that the chain would resume within 24–48 hours once mitigations are in place. IoTeX has pushed back on the highest loss estimates, saying internal calculations indicate lower net losses, but full reconciliation is pending.[^5][^3]
