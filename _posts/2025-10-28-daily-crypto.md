### **Market Overview (24h)**

Bitcoin trades at **\$114,250-\$114,500** as of 09:30 UTC (17:30 Taipei) on October 28, down **0.77-1.4%** over 24 hours after testing resistance near **\$116,000** on October 27. Ethereum sits at **\$4,090-\$4,124**, down **1.43-3.7%**, retreating from Monday's brief climb above **\$4,200**. The global crypto market cap stands at **\$3.86-3.95 trillion**, down **0.52-1.5%** in 24 hours.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6][^7]

Among top assets: **XRP** (\$2.63-2.66) is the only gainer in the top 10, up **0.4-1.3%**. **BNB** (\$1,131-1,140) fell **2.2-3.2%**, **SOL** (\$198-200) declined **0.17-0.29%**, **DOGE** (\$0.20) dropped **2.7%**, and **ADA** (\$0.67) slid **0.92%**. Notable outperformers: **HBAR** surged **16.3-18%** on ETF news, **TAO** +10%, **KERNEL** +15%.[^8][^4][^9][^10][^11][^7][^1]

**Derivatives:** BTC futures open interest climbed to **\$27.6 billion**, with funding rates positive at **0.0073%** (annualized ~7.99% on Binance), signaling gradual bullish momentum. ETH funding rate: **0.0044%**. 24-hour liquidations totaled **\$217-270 million**, with **71-76.6%** from long positions; ETH led with **\$97.7 million** in liquidations (**68.6%** longs), BTC **\$54-77 million**, SOL **\$13-34 million**.[^12][^13][^7][^14][^15]

**Stablecoin flows:** USDC supply reached **\$76.17 billion** (stable). Circle minted **750 million USDC** on Solana in recent hours, pushing Solana USDC supply above previous highs. USDT supply: **\$183.2 billion**.[^16][^10][^17][^8]

**Dominant narrative:** Market consolidation ahead of **FOMC rate decision** (Oct 29, 14:00 ET / Oct 30 02:00 UTC+8), with **96-98% probability** of 25bp cut to 3.75-4.00% range priced in. **Risk:** Medium—near-term caution before Fed decision and Powell press conference.[^4][^7][^18]

### **Security Incidents**

- **RedTiger Malware (Netskope report, Oct 27):** Open-source red teaming tool (Python-based, released 2024) weaponized into infostealer targeting gamers; steals Discord accounts, browser passwords, crypto wallets, webcam images; spread via game mods/cracked tools; uploads to GoFile; Linux/macOS variants in development. Mitigation: delete/reinstall Discord, enable 2FA, avoid unverified downloads.[^78]
- **North Korean Hackers (Google/Mandiant warning, Oct 27):** Cryptocurrency threat intelligence falling short; DPRK exploiting Web2 vulnerabilities (email, social media) rather than blockchain flaws to steal crypto for weapons programs; industry lacks sufficient defenders; blockchain transparency enables real-time tracking of stolen funds but expertise shortage persists.[^79]
- **Oracle E-Business Suite CVE-2025-61882 (FBI/CISA alert, Oct 5-28):** Critical vulnerability (CVSS 9.8) being exploited; remotely exploitable without credentials; CISA added to KEV catalog with **Oct 28 federal agency patch deadline**. Not crypto-specific but affects enterprises holding digital assets.[^80]

**No major DeFi protocol exploits or exchange hacks confirmed in Oct 27-28 window.** Chainalysis 2025 Crypto Crime Report (Jan 2025) shows 2024 illicit crypto activity at \$40.9B (lower-bound estimate, expected to rise to ~\$51B as more addresses identified).[^81][^82]
